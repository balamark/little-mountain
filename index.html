About Mark

With a strong foundation from Carnegie Mellon University and professional experience at leading tech companies like Microsoft, Mark specializes in creating innovative software solutions that drive business success.

During his time at Microsoft, Mark worked on large-scale distributed systems and gained deep expertise in enterprise-grade software development. His passion extends beyond coding to education, having taught through the TEALS Program and volunteering at the Pacific Science Center.

Mark believes in making technology accessible and empowering others through knowledge sharing, combining his industry experience with a genuine passion for teaching and mentoring.
